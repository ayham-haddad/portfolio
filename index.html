Thank you for visiting my Website. Here you'll find examples of what I do and how I reach to where I am now. Starting with my professional life growth.



I grow up in Jordan, where I started my interest in web development, as a platform that brought intellect, innovationism, and art to one stage. Though my early years of college my dreams were not sponsored through technology. At first, I started studying industrial design in college. Though I been able to do really well through the first two years of the program, my dreams were not visualized in sketches. I wanted to evolve in human relationships and business entrepreneurship, which ideally can be achieved through a joint educational program of Business administration and design. Sadly my school did not offer so. Thus, I later changed my program to Business Administration, which I graduated from on 2012 From Yarmouk University of Jordan.

During that time I had the opportunity to work with an innovative web development company, which was highly educational. The team had high expectation and the leaders gave me the chance to work on one of their big clients, through it I learned how to design and build ERP systems using raw PHP language, designing workflow, user cases, and database diagrams. Those were the stepping stones that led me to technology and web development.

I then moved to the USA to start new life experiences. I left Jordan with dreams to grow and achieve a new level of self-worth. Though I did have my online business for trade and marketing with a team in Jordan, I did need to focus on what I can make in the USA. I start working in all kind of work fields, from inside restaurants and medical equipment supply management to startups system architecture and infrastructure designs. During the last four years, I've been slowly seeking my Second Bachelor in Information Technology at UMass Boston, Which I am expected to finish next fall with the last remaining course. Yay.

And now, I am seeking to advance my knowledge and experiences in System architecture and dynamic client-focused software development. Hoping to find the right team to work with and join the team visions. .
